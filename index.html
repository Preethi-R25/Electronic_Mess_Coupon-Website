Paper-based mess coupon is a traditionalised mess coupon style in many hostels. This app is a contradict way of it. No more paper mess coupon is required in upcoming technological world.

Intro
Main motive

Papers are one of the products of trees which is not eco-friendly. Though we can't totally stop the usage of paper , we can find some ways to reduce the usage of it. In many hostels, mess coupon is given in papers. Students can face some problems of losing it or marking a wrong statement, etc. So this app is an alternised way for it.

It is an app, that is available in playstore. If any management finds it useful, they can ask their students to use it. The management needs to connect it with their server.

In case, if you lost your id card, you can use block option to block your account. It will help us from misusage or reusage of it.

Students will get an notification from the app, when the mess type selection is going to start.

Mess tab in app is used for opting your mess type for upcoming month. You can choose veg mess , metro mess or non-veg mess according to your conveninence. The procedure is to login to your account and select the type of mess you needed and click submit.

There will be a dropdown box for you to select the mess type. It will identify you whether a mess type is available or not by indicating it in different colors. For eg: Green for avalaible portion and red for non availability.

Form here refers to the reduction form. The amount for mess is paid in the beginning of the year. So if you are not using mess for certain days, we can use this form to notify that your are not using the mess.

You want to submit this form atleast before 5 hours. So it will be useful for you to reduce the amount for that particular days.

Complain box is used for showing the inconveninence of users. If you have any, you can put a complaint about that here.
